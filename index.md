# Intro to UI/UX Design

Welcome to Intro to UI/UX Design! This 2-week IAP course will serve as an introduction to UI (user interface) and UX (user experience) design, giving students the opportunity to learn design principles/methodologies and apply them to a real-world case study.

## Learning Objectives

In this class, we will follow the design thinking process throughout the course, providing lessons on user research, wireframing, low/high-fidelity prototyping, usability testing, and setting up a portfolio. Each student will be given a case study at the beginning of the course to apply the process practically and to enhance the UI/UX of a digital mobile platform. 

**Week 1** will follow a lecture and workshop format, surveying the design process and giving students the skills necessary to tackle their case studies.

**Week 2** will be structured around work sessions and individual feedback/crits and will also include talks by industry professionals. At the end of this week, each student will present their completed case study to the class and finish documenting it so that it is suitable for inclusion in a design portfolio.

Topics covered will include:
- Design thinking and process
- Figma fundamentals
- Lo-fi paper prototyping
- Hi-fi digital prototyping
- User testing
- Presenting a case study and narrative

## Grading

Students will be given credit and assigned grades based on their class participation throughout the course and their final presentations for their case study
