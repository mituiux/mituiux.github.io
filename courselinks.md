## Signing up for a Figma for Education account

**Step 1:** Create or log into your Figma account
- Head to Figma.com and click Sign up in the top right corner
- Use your MIT email via “Sign in with Google”

**Step 2:** Visit the Education status application page: https://www.figma.com/education/apply 
- Select Higher Education as your institution type
- Agree to the terms of service
- Click Next
- Complete the SheerID Form with your school and personal details
- You will need to provide pictures of a government-issued ID
- Please utilize the https://support.sheerid.com/en-US/help-center if you run into any issues

**Step 3:** Join our 6.S089 Figma team!
- https://www.figma.com/team_invite/redeem/O4M57eMg6JZJjk6R7GxhJN?t=0RbRh9I0Ahe5hkDj-21

If you have questions or need help, reach out to education@figma.com or one of the instructors or bring your problem to class!
